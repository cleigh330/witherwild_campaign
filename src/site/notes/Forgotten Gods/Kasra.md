The Forgotten God of lies, deceptions, night, and whispered secrets. His symbol was a dagger with a drop of blood off the blade.

Kasra was overtaken by [[New Gods/Dremere\|Dremere]] and forced to live in the Circles Below. Together, before his defeat, Kasra and Dremere created [[Faint Deities/Oove\|Oove]].
